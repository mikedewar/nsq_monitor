Initial Setup

  1. stand up an EC2 box in the bigwheel_consumer security group (ask Mike Dewar for the security group code).
  2. make sure you can curl bigwheel.nytlabs.com:4161/ping and queue01.bigwheel.nytlabs.com:4151/ping from your box.
  3. Write a reader in your language of choice.
  4. Stand up your reader as a daemon (we reccomend supervisord.
Important! Always name your channels yourchannelnamehere#ephemeral so that your channels will disappear when you're done.
Please get in touch with michael.dewar@nytimes.com with any questions.
